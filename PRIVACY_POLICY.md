# TREND Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 13, 2025
**Last Updated:** November 13, 2025

TREND ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, your rights, and how we protect your data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- First name and last name
- Email address
- Password (securely hashed and never stored in plain text)
- Timezone (auto-detected or manually set)
- Currency preference (auto-detected or manually set)

### Financial Data
All data you enter into TREND, including:
- Transactions (amounts, descriptions, dates, categories)
- Budget categories and allocations
- Saving goals and contributions
- Income information (pay frequency, amounts)
- Notes and descriptions you add to transactions
- Recurring transaction patterns

### Device & Security Data
- Authentication tokens (stored securely in iOS Keychain using hardware encryption)
- Biometric authentication preferences (if enabled by you)
- App lock settings and preferences

### Usage Data
- Local app performance data to maintain functionality
- No behavioral tracking or analytics services are used

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide core budgeting and expense tracking features
- Sync your data across your devices
- Calculate spending insights and patterns (processed locally on your device)
- Maintain your account security
- Send important updates about app features or policy changes (when necessary)
- Improve app functionality and fix bugs

### Important: Our "Insights" Feature
Our spending insights (such as takeout spending analysis) are calculated entirely on your device using rule-based logic. We do NOT use artificial intelligence, machine learning models, or send your financial data to any AI service providers. All analysis happens locally on your device.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Sync

### Local Storage
- Data is initially stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
- Authentication tokens are stored in iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted)
- Local caching improves app performance

### Cloud Sync
- Once you create an account and sign in, your data is automatically synced to our secure AWS servers
- Data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- Cloud sync allows you to access your data across multiple devices

### Data Security
- All passwords are securely hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- Authentication tokens are stored in iOS Keychain with hardware-level encryption
- All data transmitted to our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Input validation and sanitization protects against malicious data

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your personal or financial data to third parties.

### Service Providers
We use Amazon Web Services (AWS) to host our backend infrastructure. AWS processes and stores data on our behalf under strict contractual obligations to protect your privacy.

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

TREND does not integrate with:
- Advertising networks
- Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
- Social media platforms for tracking purposes
- Credit reporting agencies
- External AI services

We use only essential services:
- AWS (cloud infrastructure for data storage and sync)
- React Native ecosystem libraries (open-source, client-side only)

## 6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
Request a copy of all data we have about you via the "Export Data" feature in Settings.

### Delete Your Data
Permanently delete your account and all associated data:
1. Go to Settings → Account Management
2. Select "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion

Upon account deletion, all your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

### Modify Your Data
Update your profile information, transactions, budgets, and goals at any time through the app.

### Opt-Out of Communications
You can opt out of non-essential communications through Settings.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

TREND is suitable for users of all ages. Our spending insights can support financial education for younger users.

If you are under 18, we recommend using TREND with parental guidance. Parents or guardians should supervise account creation for minors.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13 without proper consent, we will delete it promptly.

---

## 8. Data Retention & Deletion

### Retention
We retain your data as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

### Deletion
When you delete your account:
- All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics (contains no personally identifiable information)
- Local cache data is immediately cleared from your device

### Logout Cache Clearing
When you log out, the app automatically clears:
- Local cached transactions and categories
- User profile data
- Goals and contributions cache
This prevents data contamination between different user accounts on shared devices.

---

## 9. Biometric Authentication

If you enable biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID):
- Your biometric data NEVER leaves your device
- We only receive confirmation of successful/failed authentication
- Biometric data is processed entirely by iOS and stored in the Secure Enclave
- You can disable biometric authentication at any time in Settings
